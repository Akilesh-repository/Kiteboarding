Wind sports are inherently weather-dependent. If the conditions aren't safe or suitable on the day of your scheduled lesson, we offer a 100% flexible rescheduling policy. You will never lose your lesson credits due to bad weather. We strongly recommend booking early in your vacation to allow buffer days.

Everyone learns at their own pace, but on average, it takes complete beginners between 9 to 12 hours of instruction to safely ride upwind, launch and land their kite, and handle the equipment independently without an instructor's supervision.
