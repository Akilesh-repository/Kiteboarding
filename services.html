Find Your Perfect Ride

We offer tailored coaching for every discipline. Whether you are stepping onto a board for the first time or looking to master unhooked loops, our specialized programs are built for rapid progression.

Level: Beginner

Zero to Hero Camp

Master the absolute basics in a safe, controlled environment. Covers wind theory, kite control, body dragging, and getting up on the board for your very first water starts.

View Details
Level: All

Wing Foil Introduction

Discover the sensation of flying above the water. This course isolates wing handling on a SUP board before introducing the hydrofoil to ensure a smooth, frustration-free learning curve.

View Details
Level: Intermediate

Upwind & Transitions

Stuck doing the "walk of shame"? This clinic fixes your posture, kite positioning, and edging to guarantee you ride consistently upwind and nail smooth, sliding turns.

View Details
Level: Advanced

Surf & Wave Clinic

Drop the twin-tip and grab a directional surfboard. Learn strapless riding techniques, wave timing, bottom turns, and how to safely navigate the impact zone in heavy surf conditions.

Level: Advanced

Big Air & Freestyle

Take your riding to the skies. Our coaches use video analysis to correct your pop and kite steering, helping you land your first jumps, backrolls, and advanced unhooked handle passes.
