Course Overview

Your Kiteboarding Journey Starts Here

The Zero to Hero camp is our signature program tailored exclusively for absolute beginners. We've stripped away the complicated jargon and built a step-by-step framework that prioritizes your safety, builds your confidence, and gets you up and riding faster than traditional methods. Over 12 action-packed hours, you'll transform from a complete novice to an independent kiteboarder.

What You'll Learn

Step-By-Step Curriculum

1. Kite Control & Safety

Learn the wind window, how to safely launch and land, and master the quick-release safety systems before ever entering the deep water. Solid kite control is the foundation of everything.

2. Body Dragging

Enter the water without the board. You'll learn how to use the kite's power to pull yourself through the water safely, and how to tack upwind to retrieve a lost board.

3. Water Starts

Bring the board into the mix. We'll teach you the exact kite positioning, power strokes, and body posture needed to pop up out of the water and take your very first ride.

Prerequisites & Gear

Everything You Need Is Included

We provide all the latest 2026 kites, boards, harnesses, impact vests, and BbTalkin radio helmets for real-time feedback. You don't need any prior watersports or board sports experience to excel in this course. The only requirement is that you are comfortable swimming in open water. Just bring your swimsuit, sunscreen, and a positive attitude!
