Who We Are

A Premier Watersports Educational Hub

SchoolKite is not just a rental shop; we are a dedicated educational facility focused on the rapid, safe, and exciting progression of kiteboarding and wing foiling. Founded by professional riders, our organization is built on the belief that high-quality coaching changes lives.

Operating year-round, our facility features state-of-the-art radio helmets, specialized jet ski support, and an exclusive beach club designed specifically for wind athletes to relax and review their daily sessions.

Our Core Purpose

Mission & Vision

Everything we do at SchoolKite is driven by a deep respect for the ocean and a desire to safely introduce others to the incredible freedom of wind sports.

Our Mission

To provide the safest, most efficient, and most enjoyable learning experience in the kiteboarding industry. We aim to break down the barriers to entry, making wind sports accessible to individuals of all ages and athletic backgrounds through innovative coaching.

Our Vision

To foster a global community of environmentally conscious riders. We envision a future where SchoolKite is recognized worldwide as the gold standard for watersports education, inspiring a lifelong passion for the ocean while promoting marine conservation.

Our Approach

Safety Is Not Optional. It's Foundational.

Our teaching philosophy revolves entirely around hazard prevention. We believe that a rider who understands the mechanics of their safety systems will learn faster because they ride without fear.

Deep Water Rescues

Every student must pass a self-rescue simulation before they are handed a board.

Weather Mastery

We teach you how to read forecasts, spot squalls, and understand local wind effects.

System Checks

Rigorous pre-flight checks to ensure quick releases and lines are perfect.

Jet Ski Backup

Our coaches are always on the water with you, ready to assist instantly.
